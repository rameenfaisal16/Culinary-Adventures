Cooking Tips & Tricks

Tip 1: Always let your meat rest

After cooking meat, let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. This helps the juices redistribute, making the meat juicier and more flavorful.

Tip 2: Use a spoon to peel ginger

Use the edge of a spoon to peel ginger. It’s easier and wastes less than using a knife.

Tip 3: Add a pinch of salt to coffee

Add a pinch of salt to your coffee to reduce bitterness.
